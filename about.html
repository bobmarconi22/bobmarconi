Who Am I?

Why I became a Software Engineer

Back in 2018 when I was a freshman in college, pursuing a degree in Business Management and Administration, I had a family friend tell me about the coding bootcamp that he was attending, I remember wondering "What the heck is a software engineer", and after about 5 minutes, and 2 total google searches later, I ironically thought "Whelp thats not for me".

Fast Forward to 2023, I was selling cars at a dealership called Gateway Kia in Warrington, PA. and in the midst of one of the slowest months of any work I had ever experienced, I found myself looking for a new job, and as a then 23 year old, I was trying to find a career. I looked up "Best Jobs for people who like to learn" and one of the top results ended up being "Web Developer". Almost immediately I thought back to that conversation in college. One phone call later, that same family friend really explained what a software engineer is, and gave me a few free sites to try my hand at it and see how I felt. After about a month of doing free online programs and learning basic HTML and JS, I entirely fell in love with it. I immediately took the next steps by enrolling in a bootcamp, I couldn't wait to start!
